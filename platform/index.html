    Macro Cards Quiz Game Platform¶

    Are you looking to build a new community quiz game? Great idea! We can help.

    Our platform enables a new, unique site (custom domain and graphics) to be produced for almost any topic with a large number of features:

    • Advanced Markdown (custom video tag, LaTex, code specific linting, copy paste images)
    • Difficulty algorithm - questions get harder as you play
    • All questions have explanations (the aim is to teach)
    • Leaderboards by country, continent, and globally (also monthly, yearly, all-time)
    • Peer review for questions & cards submitted
    • Hints and quality checks at time of submission
    • Multiplayer Mode (12 questions or "tournament" mode)
    • Public cards (indexable on google)
    • Documentation site (like this one)
    • Certificates for each level

    Roadmap¶

    We continue to invest in the platform, and the following features are in our roadmap:

    • Teams feature
    • Earn Bitcoin by submitting content
    • Earn Bitcoin by winning a quiz
    • AI feedback to further improve question quality

    Commercials:¶

    Quiz games are delivered for a fixed price of 10k Euro. This includes:

    • Production of artwork
    • Production of the first 200 seed questions
    • Social media setup, and marketing
    • Super fast image hosting
    • Powerful database and web hosting
    • 5 years of support, server hosting and domain renewals (non-premium domains only)

    Advertising revenue is shared, 50%. If this revenue covers the running costs after 5 years, then we will continue to provide hosting and domain renewals. Otherwise, we will ask for a contribution to cover our costs.

    Developer Days are available at a discounted rate of 350 euro per day for the creation of new features. We must agree that the new features are useful for all sites.
