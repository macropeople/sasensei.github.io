    Sasensei - Sponsors¶

    Whilst Sasensei is indeed a passion project for us, the fact is - it costs hard $$$ to run! So we are always on the lookout for sponsors to cover the running costs, to fund exciting new features for our players, and to ensure that sasensei.com can remain free for everyone. Forever.

    Packages¶

    Three packages are available. All sponsors are granted the right to use Sasensei branding in their own materials, for the duration of the sponsorship term. If you are interested in any of these packages, don't hesitate to contact us.

    GOLD Sponsor¶

    The GOLD sponsor is the Sasensei 'official' sponsor for one year, and gains a presence in all marketing materials and promotional events. Additional benefits:

    • Prominent Branding on the Landing Page
    • Mentioned in all newsletters
    • 2 x Global Sidebox assets
    • Unlimited Country Specific Sidebox Assets (1 per country)

    GLOBAL Sponsor¶

    Up to 3 GLOBAL Sponsors will be granted the following (for one year):

    • Branding on the Landing page
    • 1 x Global Sidebox asset
    • Up to 5 Country Specific Sidebox assets (1 per country)

    BOX Sponsor¶

    BOX sponsors will be granted the following (for minimum, 3 months):

    • Listing on the landing page
    • Up to 5 Country Specific Sidebox assets (1 per country)

    Branding Details¶

    Sidebox¶

    A small box is provided within the title bar for sponsors - note that the content posted here must be directly relevant to Sasensei players.

    Ads can be served globally, or on a country by country basis, depending on the country the user has chosen to 'represent'. Ads are refreshed every minute. Two image sizes are available - the first is 240w x 150h (mobile, smaller screens), and the second (banner ad, displayed on high resolution) is 240w x 400h.

    To ensure your advert looks good in the event of CSS changes, be sure to provide a filled (not transparent) background.

    Pricing¶

    For contracts, and pricing, please contact us.
