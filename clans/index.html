    XXX_SHORT_NAME_XXX - Clans¶

    Coming Soon!!!¶

    The documentation below describes the upcoming community functionality, to be released in phases during 2020.

    Overview¶

    The Clan feature enables players to communicate with each other via instant chat boards. Players must be at least xxx_rank2_xxx to join a Clan. Players may join up to 3 clans, one of which must be a primary clan. Additional Clans may be joined in exchange for tokens.

    Note

    Remember that this is a public site, accessible to minors and people of many alternative persuasions! The purpose of making this chat available is purely to discuss XXX_SHORT_NAME_XXX related topics. There is zero tolerance for hate speech, innuendo, harassment, trolling, or anything that would cause offence in a church, mosque, or in front of your grandma.

    Features¶

    Clan page¶

    Clans also have a profile page which explains the club, editable by xxx_rank6_xxx and above. It also shows the internal rankings. The Clan will get a default avatar, alternative images can be uploaded.

    Chat lists¶

    Discussions are held in a chat list. You can open a chat list to view new messages. Chat history is private to the site (not indexed by search engines, or visible without logging in).

    Leaderboards¶

    Every player will choose a primary Clan in settings. Players can join additional Clans, however (for leaderboard rankings) their reputation will only count towards their primary clan.

    Players will feature on the leaderboards within their primary clan. Clan leaderboards show the split by All Time / This Year / This month.

    The main site leaderboard will also be updated with a switch, so that the league tables can be by person, or by Clan.

    New Clans¶

    Joining New Clans¶

    Players are able to join up to 3 Clans by default. A player may leave a Clan to join another one. If a player has no Clans, joining a Clan makes it primary by default.

    Players may increase this capacity by purchasing Clans. There is no limit to the number of Clans a player may purchase. Each new Clan will cost 10 tokens.

    The primary clan of a player will be shown on their public profile.

    Creating New Clans¶

    New Clans can be created by anybody, for a 300 token purchase fee. The Clan Creator has automatic rights to modify the Clan profile page, regardless of rank.

    Admins (Private Clan feature)¶

    Private clans have Admins. Admins have the following powers:

    • Ability to mark other players as admins
    • Ability to update the community picture and profile
    • Ability to remove players from the community
    • Ability to delete messages

    A Super Admin has the ability to remove admins.
